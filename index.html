Villa Mandra

by

K-studio

Spirit of history. Drive modernity

Villa Mandra, a holiday home on the Greek island of Mykonos designed by K-studio, the villa looks straight out to sea and the sunset over the neighbouring island of Delos. Inspired by the humble complexity of the traditional island vernacular, The simple white volumes, the straight stone walls and light pergola planes sit comfortably in the Cycladic landscape.

Inspired by contemporary summer living

The house is built upon the idea of slow, laid-back summer living, and encourages mindful connection with family, friends and the freedom to exist peacefully in nature. Making the most of the views of the sun setting over the neighbouring island of Delos, facing the pool, the kitchen and living areas open out on to the courtyard.

Palette of natural materials

K-studio chose natural stone and wood as a key part of Villa Mandra's aesthetic. Key to the character of the house is the palette of traditional materials such as lime-wash, stone and wood that have been applied and engineered with contemporary techniques to create un-nostalgic architecture that bridges heritage and locality with contemporary life.
